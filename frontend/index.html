The Longest Race Home

Ten riders are pushing the borders of their physical limits. 7 checkpoints are spread across the country. To pass all of them, the riders will have to travel almost 400km. Will they be able to reach the finish and if so, who will reach it first?

Worried mothers, wifes and daughters can follow their heros on the map below that will display live information about the riders position, travelled distance and mental state. You can already explore the checkpoints by clicking them.
